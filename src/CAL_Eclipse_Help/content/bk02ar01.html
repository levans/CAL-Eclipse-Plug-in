Legal Notice

CAL is a strongly typed lazy functional language. The number of such languages in current development is quite small, the main ones being Haskell (http://www.haskell.org) and Clean (http://clean.cs.ru.nl).

This paper compares and contrasts CAL with Haskell and is intended for readers already familiar with Haskell.
